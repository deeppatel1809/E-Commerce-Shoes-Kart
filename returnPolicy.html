Return and Exchange Policy

1. General Return Policy

At ShoeStore, we understand that sometimes you may change your mind after purchasing a product. That's why we offer a 30-day return policy. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return the shoes within 30 days of receiving the order for a full refund.

2. Conditions for Returns

  • All returned items must be in their original, unworn, and unwashed condition.
  • Items must be returned with the original packaging and tags intact.
  • Proof of purchase (order number, receipt, etc.) is required for all returns.
  • Items that have been worn or altered in any way will not be accepted for return.

3. How to Return an Item

  1. Contact our customer service team at support@shoestore.com with your order number and the reason for the return.
  2. We will send you a return shipping label and instructions on how to return the item.
  3. Once the return is received and inspected, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days.

4. Exchange Policy

If you would like to exchange an item for a different size or color, you can do so within 30 days of purchase. Please contact our customer service team to initiate an exchange, and we will assist you with the process.

5. Refunds

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method. Please note that it may take 5-7 business days for the refund to appear in your account depending on your bank or payment provider.

6. Non-Returnable Items

For health and safety reasons, items such as socks and insoles are non-returnable unless they are defective or damaged.
